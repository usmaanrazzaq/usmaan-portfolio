Introduction

02/16/2026

Notes is a section where I write and share ideas, thoughts, research, and readings. Topics will range from product design, tech, and architecture. I'm not a skilled writer in any form, although I did well in my writing-intensive courses in college. Notes will be updated periodically; it isn't something that holds the most importance in my work. It will be used to share thoughts and ideas beyond the particular case studies written for my work.

I am a Product Designer and Design Engineer currently in the process of launching my own freelance design agency. In my day-to-day, I come across a number of applications, designs, and products; most of them I sign up for and integrate into my own workflow. Architecture is a subject that I have always held a strong interest in. Since a young age, I have always been great at drawing, sketching, and somewhat building 3D structures. Being good at such things was because of my interest in architecture and my curiosity in figuring out how they were designed and built. Although I didn't major in the subject, my interest and curiosity have never gone away. I continue to read, research, and learn about architecture outside of my profession. Thus, the idea of 'Notes' came about, where I can write about these topics that interest me. Where I can write freely and openly about applications, UI design, products, tech, and architecture. The itch to write was always there, but I never found the time to get around to starting anything. I created a Substack page to at least take the first step into bringing this idea to fruition. It's not that I lacked the motivation; I just wasn't particularly fond of the app's UI.

As mentioned earlier, Notes will be updated periodically, meaning I will write when I feel like I have something meaningful to write about. It will be built around the format of sporadic thoughts that pop into my head, and I need to write them down. Of course, that will only pertain to topics mentioned earlier in this introduction post; any other thoughts are tucked away in my private notes app. Some 'Notes' will be short and brief; some will be longer and a bit more structured.

I don't know how many people will take the time to read these or even care to read them. This is just a space for me to collect my thoughts on the topics of interest and write them out in a more structured format. In a way, this is a practice for me to maybe even be a better writer, to communicate my thoughts on these topics better, and to be more articulate. It's me talking about topics I enjoy with myself, but in a more "public" display.

I will conclude this introduction here before I continue to ramble on and have to sit through my horrible writing and make edits. I have some topics for the next post to write about, which I'll research more into and start drafting.
