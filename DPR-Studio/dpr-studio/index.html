Overview

DPR Studio is a design-driven agency fueled by curiosity and a passion for innovation. They offer tailored services in digital product design, garment design, brand identity, creative development, and digital strategy. By blending thoughtful exploration with practical expertise, DPR Studio helps partners at any stage achieve their goals through customized solutions that elevate brands and create memorable user experiences.

Work

For this project, I worked with my partner, a software engineer, through my design studio On The Run Studio to design and develop DPR Studio's new website. The founder of DPR Studio, wanted to migrate his current website from Webflow to Shopify. The studio was looking for a fresh and modern look for their new website. A custom designed interface that can be used to showcase their services and products.

We began working and designing the new website beginning of June 2025. I started off by desiging the user interface for the website. I refernced all of the website examples that I was given by the founder. I had also refernced some that I found while doing some researched and believed it matched the style the client was aiming for. I then began to design wireframes and low to high-fidelity mockups for the website on Figma. I made some funtioning prototypes for the navigation bar and a few sections of the website to show the client how these components would funtion.

After a few weeks of designing, The client was happy with one of the designs I had worked on. Currently, we are waiting for the client to provide more resources and content for the website so that we can begin developing the website.
