Overview

DPR Studio is a design-driven agency fueled by curiosity and a passion for innovation. They offer tailored services in digital product design, garment design, brand identity, creative development, and digital strategy. By blending thoughtful exploration with practical expertise, DPR Studio helps partners at any stage achieve their goals through customized solutions that elevate brands and create memorable user experiences.

Work

For this project, I collaborated with my partner, a software engineer, through my design studio, On The Run Studio, to design and develop DPR Studio's new website. The founder wanted to migrate the existing site from Webflow to Shopify and was seeking a fresh, modern look. The goal was to create a custom-designed interface that could effectively showcase their services and products.

We began the design phase in early June 2025. I started by designing the website's user interface, referencing examples provided by the founder as well as additional sites I found during my own research that aligned with the desired style. Using Figma, I created wireframes and developed low- to high-fidelity mockups. I also built functional prototypes for the navigation bar and key sections of the site to demonstrate how the components would work.

After a few weeks of designing, the client approved one of the mockups I presented. We are currently awaiting additional content and resources from the client before moving forward with development.
