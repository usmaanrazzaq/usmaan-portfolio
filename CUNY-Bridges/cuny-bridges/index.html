CUNY Bridges

Designing a job board for CUNY students.

UI Design Prototyping Mobile App
Year

2025

Timeline

1 month

Role

Product Designer & Prototyper

Platform

Figma

Overview

CUNY Bridge is an application for CUNY students who are nearing graduation that want to get setup for jobs or internships that are directly tied to their majors. CUNY Bridge allows students to signup with their CUNY student emails so that their student information, schedule, transcripts are all linked within the application to allow hiring recruiters to get an overview of their progress. Students are also able to build their resumes directly on to the app, which is showcased on their profile page.

Process

The process for this project was to design a job board for CUNY students so they can find jobs or internships that are directly tied to their majors. The application was designed to be simple and intuitive for users to navigate. Students are able to signup with their CUNY emails so that their information, schedule, transcripts are all linked within the application. This allows hiring recruiters to get an overview of their progress and qaulifications. Students are also able to build their resumes directly within the app, which is showcased on their profile page with their schedule.

Design

The overall design of the app was inspired by other job boards like Indeed, LinkedIn, and Glassdoor. I also took inspiration from social media platforms like Twitter, Threads, and Bluesky. This allowed students to connect with other students and recruiters directly through a social media like interface. The inspiration for the idea came from the fact that a lot of connections are made through social media platforms and I wanted to create a similar experience for students so they felt more connected and had an easier experience.
