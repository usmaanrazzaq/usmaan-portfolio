About

I'm a UX/UI Designer and Front-End Developer based in Queens, NY, passionate about creating intuitive, user-centered designs. I am currently pursuing my education at Borough of Manhattan Community College in Multimedia Programming and Web Design while enhancing my front-end development skills through CodeAcademy courses. I thrive on solving complex problems and crafting impactful digital experiences. My goal is to continuously improve and create designs that truly resonate with users.

I wouldn't be who I am without the inspiration of minds and creators like Dieter Rams, John Pawson, Donald Judd, Jony Ive, Dr. Abdus Salam, Anthony Bourdain, Jan Tschichold, Nas, Alvar Aalto, Peter Märkli, and many more. Their work, ideas, and spirit have shaped the way I see the world — teaching me that design, culture, and curiosity are all part of the same pursuit of meaning.
